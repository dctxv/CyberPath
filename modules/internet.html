THE INTERNET IS A RELAY RACE.

No machine knew the whole route. Each one just knew a good next step and handed the packet on — your router, DNS the phone-book, your ISP, the backbone, and finally Bob's server. Secrecy, trust, and identity aren't part of this yet. Anyone relaying the packet could read it.
SIMULATION ONLY // No live DNS, requests, scanning, interception, or probing. The route and timing are simplified; real paths have far more hops and can differ each way. Addresses shown here are reserved for documentation.
